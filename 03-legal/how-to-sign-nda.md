# How to sign and submit the NDA

Instructions for the **mutual NDA** referenced in the RFP **§5** (Materials, access, and NDA). The agreement text is in **[NDA-template.md](NDA-template.md)** — do not modify WindhorseTour’s terms; complete the vendor fields and sign.

**Point of contact:** **Harley** (WindhorseTour) — Slack only. **No email.**

---

## When you need an NDA

| Situation | NDA required? |
|-----------|----------------|
| Reading this public repo (RFP, reference files, templates) | **No** — treat materials as confidential; do not redistribute |
| Early access (repo, Figma collaborator, staging, database) before proposal | **Yes** — execute and return **before** WindhorseTour grants access |
| Submitting a proposal (due **14 days** after receiving the RFP) | **Yes** — signed mutual NDA **required** to advance |

You may sign **at any time** for early access — you do not need to wait until proposal day.

---

## Before you sign

1. Open **[NDA-template.md](NDA-template.md)** (or the PDF version WindhorseTour provides, if different).
2. Complete **all vendor fields** — typically legal entity name, registered address, jurisdiction, and authorized signatory name and title. Leave WindhorseTour fields blank for countersignature.
3. Have your **authorized signatory** review the agreement (use your counsel if required by your company).
4. Confirm the signing party matches the **entity that will contract** and invoice if selected.

---

## How to sign

1. Produce a **signed PDF** of the full mutual NDA (all pages).
2. **Wet signature** — sign, scan or photograph clearly, assemble as a single PDF — **or** **e-signature** (DocuSign, Adobe Sign, etc.) if your policy allows, exported as PDF.
3. Both parties must sign (mutual NDA). Your signed copy alone is sufficient for submission; WindhorseTour will countersign and return an executed copy when complete.
4. Do **not** submit unsigned templates, editable Word-only files, or partial pages unless Harley asks for a specific format in Slack.

---

## Where and how to submit

All submissions via **Slack** in the channel WindhorseTour opens when you are **invited to bid**.

### Option A — Early access (before proposal)

1. Upload the **signed NDA PDF** to the shared Slack channel.
2. In the same message (or thread), state: company legal name, primary contact, and whether you want **Figma collaborator**, **read-only GitHub**, **staging**, and/or **database** access (WindhorseTour confirms what is granted per RFP §5).
3. Wait for WindhorseTour **countersignature** and written confirmation of access before assuming NDA-gated materials are approved for your team.

### Option B — With your proposal (required minimum)

1. Submit your proposal PDF per RFP **§14** / **§16** by **day 14**.
2. Include the **signed mutual NDA** as a **separate PDF** in Slack, or append it to the proposal PDF — either is acceptable if the full signed agreement is included.
3. Proposals **without** a signed NDA are not advanced to the next stage.

**Do not use email, WhatsApp, or WeChat** for NDA or proposal delivery.

---

## After WindhorseTour receives your NDA

1. **Review** — Harley confirms receipt in Slack.
2. **Countersignature** — WindhorseTour signs and returns an executed copy when complete (allow **several business days**; follow up in Slack if you have not heard back within **one business week**).
3. **Access** — WindhorseTour confirms what you may access:

| Stage | Typical access after executed NDA |
|-------|-----------------------------------|
| **Finalist** (if selected after proposal review) | Read-only GitHub, Figma collaborator — WindhorseTour decides who is invited |
| **Contract** | Full repo and environments after **signed contract** + **per-person roster approval** (RFP §5) |

**No** production repo, staging, or database access exists **before** an executed NDA. Access does **not** automatically mean full write access — tier depends on stage above.
